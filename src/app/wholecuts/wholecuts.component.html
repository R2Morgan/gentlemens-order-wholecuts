ITALIAN SHOEMAKER

Andrea Buiatti

Andrea Buiatti is a 29-year-old shoe designer from Italy with a solid background in the footwear industry. His journey began in an Italian footwear and accessory company, where he worked as a CAD technician and designer.

During this time, he collaborated closely with stylists and shoe factories, gaining practical experience in the design and manufacturing processes. In 2018, he successfully completed his training as a pattern maker at the prestigious Politecnico Calzaturiero of Padua.

In November 2022, he took a significant step in his career by opening his own Showroom in Udine. This showroom serves as a dedicated space where he presents his meticulously crafted designs. Combining contemporary aesthetics with craftsmanship, he strive to offer unique and captivating footwear collections that resonate with discerning customers.
